About the App

Uzima Health

Location of our hospitals Nairobi Nakuru Kisumu Kiambu

Uzima Health is an app created by Creswave Limited that helps doctors in various branches of registered Hospitals to be able to connect with their patients with special diseases (like diabetes, pressure, heart diseases,arthritis,aids,leukimia) who are far away and be able to offer their services to their door-step or over the app. The patients can be attended to by our experienced nurses also if need be.

Our patients can also use this application to book appointments with their doctors or search and look for doctors who are specialists in various diseases. Patients can also request for our ambulance services incase of emergency.

This mobile app helps you avoid the tedious process of coming all the way to our hospital to access our services.
